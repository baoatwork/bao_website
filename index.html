Zihan Feng

Game Researcher & Designer · Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute · fengz5@rpi.com

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the critical game design program at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. The main theme of my research is to promote cross-cultural and inclusive game design. Under this theme, I work on specific research topics including serious games, interactive storytelling, and user experience design. My creations and studies are deeply inspired by my global experiences, which also offer me a diversified perspective on game research.

Hope you enjoy your stay here.
